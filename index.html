June 15, 2023

Panel: Caring for Audiovisual Data

Panel "Caring for Audiovisual Data" at the NECS 2023 Conference on June 15, 2023, 11:00 am - 12:45 pm at the University of Oslo (Blindern Campus). With contributions by Emil Stjernholm (Lund University), Isadora Campregher Paiva (Goethe University Frankfurt), Josephine Diecke (Philipps-Universität Marburg), Simon Spiegel (University of Zurich) and Sarah-Mai Dang (Philipps-Universität Marburg) and chaired by Christian Gosvig Olesen (University of Amsterdam).
